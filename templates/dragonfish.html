Black Dragonfish

Black Dragonfish This rare fish lives in the deep ocean and is known for its striking and somewhat terrifying appearance.
It has a long, slender body with a powerful jaw and needle-like teeth. The Black Dragonfish typically ranges between 15 to 30 centimeters in length.

  • Characteristics:

    • Color:

      • Often black, which helps it blend into the darkness of the deep sea.

    • Bioluminescence:

      • bioluminescent organs along its body used for attracting prey and communication.

    • Depth:

      • Inhabits depths ranging from 200 to 2,000 meters below the ocean surface.
